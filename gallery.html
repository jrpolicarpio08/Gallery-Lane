About

This website is not just a project to be included in my portfolio, this website will also showcase my photography collection that I have taken in the virtual world that I explored.

Photography can be anything we just need to look at it properly. The subject does not matter everything can be a subject in photography. that's why I created this website to show the world that no matter how bad or how good the graphics are, all we need is just the right angle and creativity to produce great photography.

With that being said I hope you enjoy exploring my small gallery and understand the perspective behind every photo i display here.

─ JRVP08
